About Read by Syllables

Read by Syllables is an educational app that helps children learn to read by visually splitting text into syllables and highlighting them with alternating colors.

Features

  • Colored syllables — automatic syllable splitting with alternating color highlighting
  • Publication library — fairy tales, poems, stories and other educational texts
  • Bilingual — full support for Polish and English
  • Personalization — syllable colors, font size, background themes
  • Custom text — enter any text and see it with colored syllables
  • Print and PDF — export materials for printing
  • Wolne Lektury content — classic Polish children's literature

Who is it for?

  • Children aged 4–8 learning to read
  • Parents supporting reading practice at home
  • Teachers looking for classroom materials
